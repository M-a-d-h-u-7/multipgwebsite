Our Story

Philosophy of Restraint

OBSIDIAN was born from a single idea: that the most powerful statement is one made in silence. Founded in 2021 in Bengaluru, we set out to create clothing that speaks to those who are unbothered by noise — who find depth in simplicity and luxury in restraint.

Every piece is designed with a single question in mind: will this still feel essential in ten years? We work with a small network of certified artisan workshops across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices at every step.

Our fabrics — organic cotton, hand-loomed linen, and recycled wool blends — are chosen for longevity over trend. We release two collections a year, each limited in run, never repeated.
